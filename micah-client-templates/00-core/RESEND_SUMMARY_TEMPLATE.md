# Resend Summary Template

Use this structure for client lead notification emails.

## Subject

New Micah Voice Lead - [Client Short Name]

## Email Body

```text
New Micah voice lead

Client:
[Client business name]

Caller:
[Caller name]

Mobile:
[Caller mobile]

Email:
[Caller email]

Reason for enquiry:
[Short reason]

Best callback time:
[Best callback time]

Urgency:
[Urgency if known]

Summary:
[Plain-English call summary]

Transcript notes:
[Important caller wording, objections, constraints, or requested next step]

Recommended next action:
[Call back / email / book appointment / escalate]
```

## Notes

- Keep the summary short and useful.
- Do not include secrets, internal prompts, or unrelated debug detail.
- Make missing fields obvious with "Not captured".
