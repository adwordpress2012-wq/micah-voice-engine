# Client Escalation Rules

## Escalate Immediately

- Caller is distressed, angry, unwell, unsafe, or urgent
- Complaint or refund request
- Legal, medical, safety, or financial concern
- Media, partnership, or supplier request
- Existing customer issue that requires staff access
- Any topic outside the approved knowledge base

## Forbidden Topics

- Prices that are not explicitly approved
- Guarantees or promises
- Internal systems or credentials
- Staff personal details
- Private customer information
- Unapproved policies

## Safe Escalation Line

> That's something the team should handle directly. I can grab your details and ask them to follow up.

## Escalation Details To Capture

- Name
- Mobile number
- Email address
- Reason for escalation
- Urgency
- Best callback time
