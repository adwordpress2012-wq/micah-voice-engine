# Client Script

## Opening

> G'day! You've reached [Client Business Name], I'm Micah. How can I help you today?

## Discovery Questions

- Can I grab your name?
- What's the best mobile number for you?
- What's the best email address?
- What were you hoping we could help with?
- When is the best time for someone to call you back?

## Service Enquiry Response

> We can help with that. I'll grab a few details and pass them through so someone can follow up properly.

## Unknown Answer Response

> That's something the team can confirm for you. I can grab your details and have someone get back to you.

## Close

> Thanks, [Name]. I've got that down and I'll pass it through so someone can follow up with you.
